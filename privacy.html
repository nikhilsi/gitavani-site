Privacy Policy

Effective: February 11, 2026

Overview

GitaVani is built with your privacy as a priority. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your reading experience is entirely private.

Data Collection

GitaVani collects no personal information. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers, no cookies, and no third-party SDKs.

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (theme, font size, language, reading progress) locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Network Access

GitaVani makes no network calls. All verse data is bundled with the app. The app works entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

GitaVani does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at support@gitavani.app.
