Support

GitaVani is a simple, offline Bhagavad Gita reader. Most features are designed to work without any setup, but if you run into issues or have suggestions, we'd love to hear from you.

Common Questions

How do I change the language?
Go to Settings (gear icon) and select Hindi or English as your default language. You can also switch languages on any verse using the toggle at the top of the translation section.

How do I change the theme or font size?
Tap the gear icon on any screen to open Settings. You'll find theme selection and a font size slider.

How do I resume where I left off?
The app automatically remembers your last read verse. A "Continue Reading" banner appears on the home screen.

Does the app need internet?
No. All verse data is bundled with the app. It works entirely offline.
